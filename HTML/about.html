About Brand

Brand Story

ReShoes was born out of our concern for waste and our commitment to creating environmentally friendly fashion. Here, we utilize recycled materials such as plastic, paper, cardboard, and more to produce high-quality shoes that are both stylish and durable. Every product from ReShoes is a fusion of innovative design, dedicated to reducing the negative impact of the fashion industry on the environment. By transforming waste into valuable products, we strive to inspire people to support a more sustainable and eco-friendly lifestyle.

Vision

Becoming a part of the fashion industry in Indonesia by transforming waste into high-quality, environmentally friendly shoes, while also inspiring the global community, especially in Indonesia, to contribute to the preservation of our planet.

Mission

  • Reducing the impact of waste and other recycled materials by managing them into shoes
  • Educating the public about the importance of choosing environmentally friendly fashion
  • Developing new innovations in domestic shoe design that combine modern style with recycled materials

Did You Know?

In 2023, paper/cardboard, plastic, rubber/leather, and fabric waste contributed significantly to global waste. Paper and cardboard made up 10.86%, yet much of it still ends up in landfills despite being easy to recycle. Plastic accounted for 19.19 %, posing a major environmental threat due to its slow decomposition and low recycling rates. Rubber and leather waste, at 2.55%, often from tires and household items, is difficult to recycle. Fabric waste, largely from the fashion industry, contributed 2.95%, and despite efforts, much of it is still not recycled.
